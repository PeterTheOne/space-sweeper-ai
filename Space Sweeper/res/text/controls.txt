GAME CONTROLS


Use <left>, <right> and <up> keys to steer ship.

Press <left-ctrl> to fire bullet, <left-shift> to launch missile.

Press <F11> to toggle full-screen mode. Press <F10> to toggle vsync.

Use <p> to pause the game. Press <F1> to toggle in-game console.

<ESC> aborts the game and brings you back to the main menu.
